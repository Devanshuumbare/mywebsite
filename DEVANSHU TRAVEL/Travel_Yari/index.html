RATANGAD,BHANDARDARA

Ratangad (Marathi: रतनगड) is a fort in Ratan Wadi, Maharashtra, India, overlooking the locale of Bhandardara, one of the oldest artificial catchment area. This fort is located in the Ahmadnagar district of Maharashtra. It is on the boundary of Ahmadnagar and Thane districts

KALAVANTINI DURG,PANVEL

Perched at a height of around 2,250 feet, the Kalavantin Durg is one of the steepest fortresses in the Western Ghats of Maharashtra. Located between Panvel and Matheran, this fort was built during the rule of the Bahmani Sultanate

AMK DURG,AKOLE

The trek to Alang fort is known as Alang Malang Kulang Trek(AMK Trek).This Trek is known as one of the most difficult treks in the region of Maharashtra. The three forts are regarded as the toughest to be reached.It has a temple of Lord Shiva.
